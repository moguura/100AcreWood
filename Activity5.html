Activity 5

Do you remember さくら? It's the next day now, and さくら is telling her friends about yesterday. You're going to be さくら and talk about what happened yesterday.
Let's try an example. Press the microphone button and say,
てんきは よかったです。 or just, よかったです。

e.g.

いい → てんきは（ 　　　）

Since yesterday's weather was nice, the answer is,
てんきは よかったです。(The weather was good.)
Now, be さくら and tell your friends about yesterday like in the example.

You can speak into the microphone just once.
