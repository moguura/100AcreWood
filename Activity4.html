Activity 4

あやめ was born in Japan but grew up in Australia.
She now lives in Japan.
She first thinks in English and then speaks in Japanese.

Let's try Example 1. Please press the microphone button and say
えいがは よかったです。 or よかったです。

　

In Example 1, あやめ was thinking "The movie review was positive."
in her head. The Japanese she spoke was えいがは よかったです。

Now, let's try Example 2. Please press the microphone button and say
えいがは よくなかったです。 or よくなかったです。

　

In Example 2, あやめ was thinking "The movie review was not positive."
in her head. The Japanese she spoke was えいがは よくなかったです。
