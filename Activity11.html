Now, let's play a game comparing まえ (before) and いま (now).
Start by looking at the image. Then press the play button to listen to the audio, and after that, press the microphone button to give your answer.

Let's try Example 1.

e.g.1

Please talk about the present. "いまは～"

　　

You heard the sentence, まえは あたらしかったです。 "Before, it was new." Since you heard まえ (before), you should talk about いま (now). So, your response should be, いまは あたらしくないです。"Now, it's not new."

Next, let's try Example 2.

　

e.g.2

Please talk about the past. "まえは～"

You heard, いまは ちかいです。 "Now, it is close." Since you heard いま (now), you should talk about まえ (before).
So, your answer should be, まえは ちかくなかったです。
"Before, it wasn't close."
