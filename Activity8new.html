Activity 8

Last week, you went on a three-day trip. Here are your impressions of the trip:

The weather was good.
But your suitcase was heavy.
The hotel was nice.
The bed in the hotel was big.
But the hot spring was not warm.
There were many people in the hot spring.
You went to the mountain, but the mountain was not fun.
The hiking was not cool.
You went to a restaurant, but the restaurant was not close to the hiking spot.
But the fish was cheap.
And it was delicious.

Today, you met your classmate さくら.

さくら wants to hear about your trip.
Let's try an example. さくら will ask a question in Japanese. After pressing the play button to listen to the audio, press the microphone button and say either
てんきはよかったです or よかったです｡

　 　　

e.g.

The weather was good.😊👍

いい → てんきは（ 　　　）

　　

The English sentence is 'The weather was good.' Therefore, てんきはよかったです is the correct answer. Please continue talking with さくら about your trip in the same way as the example.

You can speak into the microphone just once.
